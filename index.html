Welcome to SmartValle

A luxury smart villa nestled in the breathtaking mountains of Kandy, Sri Lanka. Located just a short distance from renowned attractions like the Royal Botanical Garden, Temple of the Tooth Relic, and Hanthana Mountains, Deltota mountains where the first Ceylon Tea plantations were established, SmartValle offers guests an unmatched experience that combines natural beauty with state-of-the-art smart home technology. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or an adventurous escape, SmartValle offers it all. Our villa is fully equipped with smart kitchens, automated bathrooms, smart security systems, and luxury amenities that ensure your stay is both comfortable and unforgettable. Enjoy hiking, surfing including training, and outbound training activities, or simply relax with panoramic mountain views.
